Windows And Doors Repairs

I'm an independent double glazing repair specialist, who offers a repair service that most window companies are not able to offer, at a fraction of the cost of new double glazed windows and doors.

Schedule a Free Estimate

Repairs

Window Handles / Door Handles

We repair and replace multipoint locks to ensure smooth operation and security. Ideal for doors that are hard to lock or won't close properly.

Misted Units / Blown Glass / Condensation

We replace failed double-glazed units to remove fogging and heat loss. A cost-effective solution without changing the full frame.

Euro Cylinders & Lock Barrels

We fit and replace euro cylinders, including anti-snap security upgrades. Improves protection against break-ins and key snapping.

Letterbox Fitting

We neatly fit letterboxes into doors and panels without damage. Clean finishing with correct sizing and alignment.

Dropped Doors & Windows Repair

We realign dropped doors and windows for proper closing and security. Prevents scraping, drafts, and further damage.

Extra Security Solutions

We install additional locks and security upgrades where needed. Tailored solutions for homes and businesses.

Installations

PVC Windows

We supply, repair, and maintain PVC windows for durability and efficiency. Improves insulation, security, and overall appearance.

Composite Doors

We install and repair composite doors for strength and style. Designed for high security, low maintenance, and long-lasting performance.
